Our Mission

Mental health and its related issues have been on the rise over
the years. There is a dire need to provide mental health services
free from stigmatization and at an affordable rate that enhances
ease of access. Mind Tag provides a solution by providing a
platform that creates a safe community for individuals to freely
express themselves without the fear of judgment and also
offering access to mental health practitioners and materials.
